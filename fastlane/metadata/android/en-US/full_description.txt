Alternative homescreen/launcher which features a side menu used to automatically and/or manually organize your apps into common-sense and customizable categories. It also includes appwidget support, text search for apps, a QuickBar, homescreen shortcuts/links, recent apps list, UI color configurations, Adw/Nova/Apex iconpacks, icon and app label customization, icon tinting, built-in themes, Android 7.1+ action and pinned shortcuts, and portrait and landscape support.

Version 0.8 introduces a hideable menu, animated transitions, some new config options, and speed-ups and bugfixes

Version 0.8.2 introduces resizeable widgets, appinfo, better action menus, and shortcuts that work.

Features:
Configurable app categories in a scrollable/hideable side menu.

Scrolling QuickBar on the bottom for easy access to your favorite apps.

Text search to find apps.

List of recent apps.

Multiple widget support.

Rearrange/sort icons and menu items.

Shortcut/link and Oreo Pinned shortcut support.

Android 7.1+ shortcut actions.

Unread badges.

Adw/nova/apex icon-packs.

Supports both portrait and landscape mode.

Can hide apps you don't want but can't uninstall.

Back up and restore your settings.

Configurable colors.

Customize icons and labels.

Built-in themes.
